About

A quick snapshot of what I ship and how I work.

What I do

I build gameplay features that are designer-friendly, production-ready, and stable under iteration. My sweet spot is the intersection of systems, moment-to-moment feel, and tooling.

  • Gameplay systems: quests, dialogue, progression & meta systems
  • Player-facing features: 3C, combat, UI/tutorial flows
  • Platforms shipped: Meta Quest, PlayStation 5 support, WebGL
  • Strengths: prototype → production, debugging, iteration with designers

How I work

  • Start with intent: define player goal, constraints, and success criteria
  • Prototype fast: validate the loop, then harden the architecture
  • Design for iteration: data-driven knobs, clear tools, low friction
  • Ship-ready: performance, edge cases, and maintainability

I keep collaboration simple: clear communication, clean handoffs, predictable delivery.
