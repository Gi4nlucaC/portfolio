About

A quick snapshot of what I do and how I work.

What I do

I design and implement gameplay systems that teams can iterate on quickly. I care about clarity (tools and workflows), feel (moment-to-moment gameplay), and reliability (features that scale).

  • Focus: gameplay systems, quests & narrative, tooling
  • Experience: VR (Meta Quest) and PSVR porting
  • Strengths: prototyping → production, debugging, iteration with designers

How I work

  • Start with intent: define player goal, constraints, and success criteria
  • Prototype fast: validate the loop, then harden the architecture
  • Design for iteration: data-driven knobs, clear tools, low friction
  • Ship-ready: performance, edge cases, and maintainability

I aim to be a low-maintenance collaborator: clear communication, clean handoffs.
